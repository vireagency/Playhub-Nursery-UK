Welcome to Playhub Day Nursery

Where Little Dreams Take Flight

We are an OFSTED registered private day care delivering care and education for children aged 0- 11 years. Our mission is to consistently provide excellent and quality educational, emotional and physical needs for all children in our care. The nursery is open all year round excluding the week between Christmas and New Year.

Services

Our Bright and Exciting Learning Experience

We Provide a Nurturing Environment Where Fun, Growth, and Learning Come Together to Inspire Young Minds Every Day!

Exciting Curriculum

A rich curriculum aligned with the EYFS framework, focusing on play-based learning to spark curiosity, critical thinking, and a love for exploration.

Caring Team

Our experienced early years practitioners nurture each child’s interests and design activities to support their growth and learning.

Inclusive Practice

We support equality, independence, self-help skills, and help build children’s confidence in a caring, inclusive environment.

Parent & Community Partnership

We work closely with parents and the community, including strong links to local mainstream schools, to support your child's journey.
